To promote and increase food growing and biodiversity in Bradford

To promote resource and information sharing and a spirit of cooperation in order for a variety of organisations and individuals to work together to best effect in promoting and increasing food growing and biodiversity in Bradford

Grow Bradford engages in networking and documenting food growing projects and activities, and those linked projects and activities that improve biodiversity

Grow Bradford is also involved in practical permaculture, food growing initiatives in order to engage more people in such activities and to create more spaces and practitioners to work in such ways.

Grow Bradford emphasizes using art and creativity to engage people and promote information through a website and in other venues and activities.
